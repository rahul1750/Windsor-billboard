Overview

This project is an interactive map web application that is mainly concerned with the daily
updates of Windsor-Essex. With the help of this web application, we will be able to know about
the daily mishappenings in the city displayed on the map. Besides that, we also will implement
a function to display events that are held in Windsor-Essex. For example, any kind of concert or
sports events.

Motivation

The motivation of this project is to acknowledge the people who live in Windsor-Essex about
incidents that happened in that area. Moreover, it will also encourage the residents of
Windsor-Essex to make entry of any such events near them on the map.

Business Goals

In any business, creating customers is the biggest priority. After our product gets launched and our
first potential customers, that is the people of Windsor-Essex, get accustomed to using it and give us
feedback, our goal is to reach more customers in cities around Windsor by extending our map. Additionally,
we also want to grab the attention of event managers for using our software to advertise about any ongoing
event around the city, like a concert or all kinds of sports.
